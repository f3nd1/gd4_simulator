# Regulatory References — GD4 EduTrust Specific Clauses

When you raise a finding, cite the **exact regulatory provision** being breached, not just the GD4 item number. Generic "EduTrust requires…" is not acceptable. Use the references below.

## Criterion 1 — Corporate Governance & Financial Management
- **Companies Act (Cap. 50)** — annual external audit obligation for companies above the audit exemption threshold; director's duty of care.
- **SSG PEI Registration Conditions** — principal officer (Key Executive Officer) must be fit and proper; change of KEO requires SSG notification within 30 days.
- **MOM Employment Act** — HR policies (leave, hours, contracts) must comply with EA minimums.

## Criterion 2 — Corporate Administration (HR / Marketing / Data)
- **PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act 2012, as amended 2021)** — written data-protection policy required; consent management; mandatory 3-day breach notification to PDPC for significant breaches. Absence of a PDPA-compliant data-protection policy is a statutory breach, not just an audit gap.
- **SSG PEI Act s.35** — PEI must not make false or misleading representations in advertising. Any unsubstantiated employment or outcome claim in marketing is a potential PEI Act breach.
- **MOM Fair Consideration Framework** — job advertising and recruitment practices.
- **Teacher qualifications**: ACTA/ACLP for WSQ programmes; relevant degree/diploma for academic programmes. Staff below the required qualification are a compliance gap, not just a best-practice gap.

## Criterion 3 — External Agents
- **CPE Agent Registration Framework** — agreements with overseas agents must include anti-misrepresentation clauses. An agent recruiting students without a signed agreement is a control failure referencing PEI Registration Conditions.

## Criterion 4 — Student Protection (HIGHEST REGULATORY DENSITY)
- **Standard Student Contract (SSC)** — SSG-prescribed template (updated 2025). Any deviation requires prior SSG approval. Non-standard clauses are a regulatory breach.
- **Fee Protection Scheme (FPS)** — 100% of fees must be FPS-covered from the time of collection (Insurance or Escrow). Cite the specific FPS instrument (policy number / escrow account). Any uncovered amount, even briefly, is a breach.
- **Refund Policy** — mandatory minimum refund table prescribed by SSG. Late refund = breach. Calculate the statutory due date and state the actual payment date.
- **Cooling-off period** — 7 working days from contract date; full refund if student withdraws within this window regardless of course start.
- **International student welfare** — ICA Student Pass requirements: PEI must monitor attendance (90% minimum for Student Pass holders) and report non-compliance to ICA within 3 working days of triggering the threshold.

## Criterion 5 — Academic Systems
- **National Framework of Qualifications (NFQ)** / **SSG WSQ** — programmes must be mapped and approved at the correct qualification level. Teaching staff qualifications must match the approved programme at that NFQ level.
- **Assessment moderation**: SSG/EduTrust guidance requires internal moderation for every summative assessment. External moderation is required for qualifications above Level 3.

## Criterion 6 — Quality Assurance
- **ISO 9001:2015 cl. 9.2** (for ISO-certified institutions) — internal audit must be planned, independent, and cover all processes over the cycle. Zero findings across the whole system is a red flag.
- **EduTrust GD4 §23 APSR rubric** — Management Review must be scheduled, documented, and review ALL standard inputs (KPIs, audit findings, feedback, resource adequacy).

## Criterion 7 — Performance Outcomes
- **EduTrust GD4 §7** — four mandatory 7.2 aspects must all be present and evidenced. A missing aspect is a coverage gap regardless of overall score.
- **Employment outcome claims** — must match SSG graduate employment survey methodology; response rate must be stated. Employment data that cannot be traced to the survey instrument is unsubstantiated.

## How to cite in a finding
Write: "This constitutes a breach of [Act/Provision] and GD4 [item]. The institution must [specific action] to comply."
