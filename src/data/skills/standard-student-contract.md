# Standard Student Contract (SSC) requirements (Criterion 4.2.1)

The student contract is a zero-tolerance area: it is the legal instrument SSG standardises, and contract findings are close to automatic in real assessments. Audit the PEI's contract PRACTICE against these checks — every one has produced real AFIs at Singapore PEIs, several at this PEI specifically.

## Sequence rules (the hard gates)

- **Sign before (or at the same time as) collecting.** No Course Fees or Miscellaneous Fees may be collected before the student contract is executed. The classic red flag: a **receipt or fee record dated earlier than the contract signature date**. Whenever both dates are visible, COMPARE THEM and state the comparison explicitly.
- **Advisory Note (Form 12) BEFORE signing.** The student must acknowledge the Advisory Note to Students before signing the contract, and the acknowledgement must be evidenced (signed/dated). *2025 AFI at this PEI: a named student's file showed no Form 12 acknowledgement before contract signing.* When sampling student files, check the Form 12 acknowledgement exists and pre-dates or matches the contract date.
- **Current version.** The contract used must be the current SSG version (check version identifiers/dates on the template against what the PPD says is in force). An old template still in circulation is a finding.

## Content that must be present and internally consistent

- Course details: title, duration, commencement date, qualification awarded, developer/awarding body.
- **Schedule B/C fee schedules**: itemised Course Fees and Miscellaneous Fees with amounts and payment schedule. Cross-check: the amounts on actual receipts must match the contract schedules.
- **Schedule D refund table** and the cooling-off clause (see the refund-rules skill — the cooling-off refund is FULL, not a Schedule D percentage).
- FPS particulars: the adopted scheme and the protection arrangements for the fees in Schedule B.
- Dispute-resolution avenue (e.g. CPE Student Services Centre mediation-arbitration / Small Claims Tribunal route).

## Execution quality

- Signed by BOTH parties, dated, with the authorised signatory identifiable; for minors, the parent/guardian signs.
- Every enrolled student in the sample period has a contract — reconcile enrolment records against contract records and report the counts ("X of Y sampled enrolments had an executed SSC").
- Amendments (course changes, deferments, transfers) executed as contract variations or NEW contracts — never handwritten edits on the old one. **An approved transfer requires a new SSC** (a documented gap at this PEI in 2025).
- Contract language the student understands; where a translated version is used, both versions are retained.

## Cross-document consistency checks (run these every time)

1. PPD contract procedure ↔ actual executed contracts (does practice follow the documented steps and sequence?).
2. Contract fee schedules ↔ receipts/invoices in the evidence.
3. Contract refund/cooling-off clauses ↔ PPD refund section ↔ handbook/website.
4. Contract version stated in the PPD ↔ version actually signed in the sampled files.

## Register for negatives

"It was not evident that the PEI had ensured [students acknowledged the Advisory Note in Form 12 before signing / the contract was executed before fee collection / …]. Example: [named student / contract ref, dates compared]." A contract negative without a named example (student, contract ref, or dated document) is unsupported — name what was sampled and what was absent.
