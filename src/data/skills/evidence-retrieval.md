# Evidence Retrieval Skill

## Retrieve chunks by checklist line and APSR dimension

When assessing a checklist line, retrieve the most relevant chunks for each APSR dimension. Do not use any chunk unless it directly supports the specific dimension being assessed.

## Per-dimension retrieval guidance

**Approach** — retrieve policy/procedure chunks:
- Look for: policy documents, SOPs, frameworks, procedures, guidelines, handbooks
- Preferred: specific to this requirement (not a generic policy that mentions it in passing)
- Preferred: institution-specific (not a boilerplate template)
- Preferred: includes who is responsible, when it is done, and how it is done

**Processes** — retrieve implementation record chunks:
- Look for: logs, registers, forms with data filled in, screenshots of systems, attendance records, completed checklists, dated reports
- Preferred: dated records from the current audit period (not prior years)
- Preferred: named roles or individuals carrying out the process
- Preferred: records that show the specific process referenced in the checklist line

**Systems & Outcomes** — retrieve data, survey analysis, trend reports, KPI chunks:
- Look for: aggregated outcome data, trend charts, satisfaction survey results, pass rates, KPI dashboards, comparison to target
- Preferred: outcome data with a target or baseline to compare against
- Preferred: data covering the full audit period (not a single snapshot)

**Review** — retrieve minutes, decisions, action trackers, improvement records:
- Look for: management review minutes with specific decisions, quality review records, action item trackers showing closure, process improvement documentation
- Preferred: minutes that show what was DECIDED (not just "discussed")
- Preferred: action items with owners and closure status

## Rules for chunk selection

- **Prefer specific over generic**: A chunk that directly addresses this checklist line's requirement is stronger than a chunk that mentions the topic in passing.
- **Prefer dated over undated**: An undated document cannot prove the process happened in the audit period.
- **Prefer audit-period evidence over stale**: Evidence from 3+ years ago does not prove current compliance.
- **If no relevant chunk exists for a dimension**: Rate that dimension as "Not evident" and state: "No relevant evidence chunk was found for this dimension."
- **Do not use unrelated chunks**: If the only chunks available mention similar words but are about a different process or requirement, do not cite them. Word overlap is not evidence of relevance.
