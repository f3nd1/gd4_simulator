Three ways to get from Evidence Folder to Final Report
⚠ This is the PROPOSED / TARGET flow you are designing — not what the code does today. Today's Hybrid pauses at each step going forward; the version below runs a full draft first, then lets you iterate. This diagram doubles as the spec for building it.

⚡ Full Auto — one-way trip, don't look back

Click once at the start. Everything runs to a finished Final Report with no stops — including the band. A disclaimer makes clear the AI's verdicts and scores are a simulation, not an official SSG result, and yours to override.

🔄 Hybrid — your usual mode: fast full draft, then iterate

First pass is just like Full Auto — one click gives you a complete draft all the way to the Final Report. Then you loop back into any module, adjust, re-run, and re-land on the report as many times as you like until you're happy. Human judgement lives in the refinement, not in blocking each step.

✍️ Full Manual — you build every step by hand from the start

Nothing writes to the checklist or raises a finding by itself. AI can suggest on request, but every verdict, finding and band is entered by you. Unchanged from today.

Evidence Folder

AI results, if run, stay as on-screen suggestions only — never auto-commit.
↓

↓

Always fully manual
Band auto-scored (Full Auto only)
The band (6×4 matrix) lives on the Sub-Criterion Checklist → each item → "APSR band — per-dimension percentage matrix" section. In the target design it auto-scores in Full Auto and in the Hybrid first draft; it stays fully manual in Manual mode; and in every mode you can always re-open it and override. Text extraction and score/report recalculation always run in the background regardless of mode — that's infrastructure, not a judgement call.
